Southern Region is one of the most famous and large-scale map mods, dedicated to the detailed recreation of the Southern Federal District of Russia. Immerse yourself in the unique atmosphere of the Russian South, from the bustling streets of Rostov-on-Don to the scenic coastlines of the Black Sea.

Key Features:

  • Vast Territory: Thousands of kilometers of real-world roads.
  • Dozens of Cities: Includes major cities like Rostov-on-Don, Krasnodar, Sochi, and many smaller towns.
  • High Detail: Custom models, realistic road infrastructure, and authentic road signs.
  • Atmospheric Environment: A true-to-life feeling of the region.

Project Status:

The main author of the map, SimKA, is currently taking a break from development. Map maintenance and updates are temporarily handled by createdbaness. This version is published on the site with the official permission of the author.

Required DLCs:

All official map DLCs are required for the map to work correctly.
